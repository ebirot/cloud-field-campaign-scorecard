🟢 Highlights
Strong MDP cont. from BDR w/ double-digit growth in Central (+32% Y/Y), South (+28% Y/Y), and France (+11% Y/Y). MDP cont. from Digital Specialist ramping up with $16.4M generated across OUs. Significant growth from ECS (+133% Y/Y) in North, despite being down overall.

Decline in organic (-48% Y/Y) and paid (-31% Y/Y) search as well as organic (-41% Y/Y) and paid (-13% Y/Y) social
Despite +28% Y/Y growth in MDP from webinars, Central MDP is down -16.6% Y/Y fueled by -23% Y/Y decline in Events and -14% Y/Y decline in “other” email and organic offers. Moderate Y/Y growth in South can be attributed to a -22% Y/Y decline in Events.
Webinar: Continue webinar momentum with execution of (4) Q2 webinars and live and on-demand promotion through DSE and Nurtures; promote (1) Q2 webinar on-demand via CFM Paid Media pilot
Email: Activate Q2 GIC BOM, including 7 UK English offers, 3 French offers, 3 German offers, 3 Italian offers, 3 Dutch offers, and 3 Spanish (Spain) offers; continue monitoring offer mix of demos, guides, etc.
Events: Build on CNX’26 momentum with Post-CNX’26 webinar and follow-up nurture targeting attendees and non-attendees w/ CNX2U content
Organic & Paid Search: Follow-up w/ paid media team on decline in SEM

LEAD : Jordan

Problem Statement
A number of Org62 Campaigns with a Parent Tactic of ‘Email’ are incorrectly getting attributed with MDP, causing inflated numbers for the email channel. This typically occurs when marketers use the ‘Campaign Entry’ mechanism for Nurture Quests and load their target audience to a Name Capture Campaign with incorrect statuses such as ‘Responded’. This leads to two undesired outcomes:
MDP: The ‘Campaign Entry’ Org62 Campaign designed to build the target audience list is getting MDP attribution, despite most of the audience not engaging with the actual offers in the Nurture Quest. Examples of these Campaigns below.
Health of funnel: When Lead records get added to these Campaigns, the Offer Type Most Recent field on the Lead record gets updated with whatever Offer Type value is in the Campaign which impacts Health of Funnel Reporting in the Leads Cockpit


Proposed Solution
Existing Campaigns
Change Campaign Member status for all Campaign Members in identified Campaigns to ‘Sent’ which is a status that doesn’t qualify for MDP. Will this retroactively disqualify the identified Campaigns or is too late?

New Campaigns

Propose to add Campaign Members to a ‘Generic Campaign’. Generic Campaigns do not qualify for MDP and also do not require an ‘Offer Type’ value which will also help improve Health of Funnel reporting.
